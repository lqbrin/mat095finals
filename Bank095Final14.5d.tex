}

	$\dfrac{x^4x^2z^5}{y^3}$ & 1 pt each for variables $y$ and $z$ (with positive exponent)\\ \\
	$\dfrac{x^6z^5}{y^3}$ & 2 pts for getting exponent of $x$ correct\\
	& and 1 pt extra for getting it all correct.
	\end{tabular}
}
